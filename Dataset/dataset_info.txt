Original Purpose:
This dataset is intended for customer churn prediction in a banking context.
It includes customer demographic and financial attributes such as credit score,
geography, gender, age, tenure, balance, number of products, and activity status.
The objective is to classify whether a customer exits (churns) or remains.
It is widely used for binary classification tasks in machine learning.
